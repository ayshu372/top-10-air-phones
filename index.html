Top 10 Airphones: Best Wireless Earbuds of 2024

1. Apple AirPods Pro (2nd Generation)

Apple’s AirPods Pro (2nd Generation) are widely regardedApple’s AirPods Pro (2nd Generation) are widely regarded as some of the best true wireless earbuds on the market. Offering industry-leading Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) and Transparency Mode, they provide a truly immersive audio experience. The H2 chip ensures high-fidelity sound, and the customizable fit (with different sizes of silicone tips) adds to their comfort. The spatial audio feature also enhances movie and music experiences.

  • Key Features: ANC, Transparency Mode, Spatial Audio, 6 hours of battery life, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Apple users, active noise cancellation.

2. Sony WF-1000XM5

Sony’s WF-1000XM5 are designed for those who value premium sound and noise isolation. Featuring Sony’s best-in-class ANC, these earbuds deliver exceptional sound quality with deep bass and clear highs. They are equipped with multiple microphones for improved voice quality during calls and come with a sleek design.

  • Key Features: ANC, custom sound settings, 8 hours of battery life, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Audiophiles, travelers, anyone looking for superior noise cancellation.

3. Bose QuietComfort Earbuds II

Bose has long been a leader in the noise-canceling space, and the QuietComfort Earbuds II are no exception. With their advanced ANC technology, they can block out even the most persistent background noise, making them perfect for flights or crowded areas. The earbuds also feature rich, clear audio and comfortable fit for extended wear.

  • Key Features: ANC, transparent mode, 6 hours of battery life, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Frequent travelers, noise isolation enthusiasts.

4. Jabra Elite 85t

Jabra’s Elite 85t earbuds combine superb sound quality with an adjustable ANC feature. The 6-microphone array ensures clear calls, while the IPX4-rated design makes them perfect for workouts or outdoor activities. With wireless charging, these earbuds are not only functional but also highly convenient.

  • Key Features: Adjustable ANC, 5.5 hours of battery life, wireless charging, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Active individuals, work calls, balanced sound quality.

5. Samsung Galaxy Buds Pro 2

Samsung’s Galaxy Buds Pro 2 are the top choice for Android users. Offering premium sound quality, impressive ANC, and excellent microphone performance, they deliver a great overall experience. They also support 360 Audio for immersive surround sound when paired with compatible devices.

  • Key Features: ANC, 8 hours of battery life, 360 Audio, IPX7 water resistance.
  • Best For: Samsung Galaxy users, immersive audio experiences.

6. Beats Fit Pro

For those who are active and love high-energy sound, the Beats Fit Pro are a great choice. These earbuds provide powerful sound with deep bass, excellent fit for exercise, and secure wings to keep them in place during movement. They also feature ANC and spatial audio, making them a top choice for workouts and music lovers.

  • Key Features: ANC, spatial audio, 6 hours of battery life, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Athletes, gym-goers, people seeking high bass sound.

7. Sennheiser Momentum True Wireless 3

Sennheiser’s Momentum True Wireless 3 earbuds offer superior sound quality with aptX Adaptive and AAC support. They come with adaptive ANC, which adjusts the level of noise cancellation based on the environment. The premium design and comfortable fit make them perfect for long listening sessions.

  • Key Features: ANC, superior sound quality, 7 hours of battery life, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Audiophiles, long listening sessions, sound quality seekers.

8. Google Pixel Buds Pro

Google’s Pixel Buds Pro are the best choice for those who want seamless integration with Google devices and services. These earbuds feature ANC, multi-device pairing, and impressive sound quality for all types of music genres. The comfort fit and long battery life ensure that they are great for extended use.

  • Key Features: ANC, 11 hours of battery life, fast charging, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Google ecosystem users, long-lasting earbuds.

9. Anker Soundcore Liberty 4

For budget-conscious buyers, Anker’s Soundcore Liberty 4 earbuds are an excellent option. Offering premium features like ANC, sound customization, and great battery life (up to 9 hours), these earbuds offer excellent value for the price.

  • Key Features: ANC, customizable sound, 9 hours of battery life, IPX4 water resistance.
  • Best For: Budget buyers, users who value good sound on a budget.

10. Nothing Ear (2)

The Nothing Ear (2) earbuds offer an innovative design with crystal-clear sound, ANC, and high-end features at a more affordable price. The unique transparent design and great app functionality make them stand out in the crowded wireless earbuds market.

  • Key Features: ANC, transparency mode, 6 hours of battery life, IP54 water resistance.
  • Best For: Design lovers, those seeking value without compromise.
